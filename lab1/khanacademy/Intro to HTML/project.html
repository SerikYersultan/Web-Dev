Travel to Kazakhstan

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Kazakhstan, where vast steppes meet majestic mountains. Experience the rich culture, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality of the Kazakh people.

Places to Visit

  • Almaty - The largest city, known for its vibrant nightlife and stunning parks.
  • Astana (Nur-Sultan) - The modern capital with futuristic architecture.
  • Charyn Canyon - A natural wonder that rivals the Grand Canyon.
  • Baikonur Cosmodrome - The world's first and largest space launch facility.

Things to See

  1. The Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi - A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  2. The Great Steppe - Experience the vastness of Kazakhstan's natural landscapes.
  3. Lake Balkhash - A unique lake with both salty and fresh water.

Local Cuisine

Indulge in traditional Kazakh dishes such as Beshbarmak (boiled meat with pasta), Plov (rice pilaf), and Kazy (horse meat sausage). Don't forget to try the local tea and fermented mare's milk, known as Kumys.
